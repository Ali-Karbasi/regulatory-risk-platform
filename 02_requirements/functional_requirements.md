# Functional Requirements

## FR-1:
  user must be able to create and update regulatory records, including jurisdiction, regulation name, effective date, and regulatory category.

## FR_2:
user must be able to log privacy and compliance risks and associate them with one or more regulatory requirements.

## FR-3:
each risk must be assigned a business unit, risk owner, and compliance status.

## FR-4:
the system must calculate risk severity using standardized scoring logic based on impact and likelihood.

## FR-5:
the system must flag high-risk and non-compliance records based on defined thresholds.

## FR-6:
the system must support executive dashboards displaying KPIs, trends, and exception-base reporting.
